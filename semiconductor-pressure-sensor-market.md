<p><strong>The Semiconductor Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive Sensors</li><li>PN Junction Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor pressure sensor market is primarily characterized by two types: piezoresistive sensors and PN junction sensors. Piezoresistive sensors measure pressure by detecting changes in electrical resistance caused by deformation of a semiconductor material under pressure. They are widely used in various applications due to their high sensitivity and accuracy. PN junction sensors, on the other hand, utilize the voltage change across a semiconductor junction in response to pressure variations. These sensors are known for their robustness and are often used in industrial environments.</p></p>
