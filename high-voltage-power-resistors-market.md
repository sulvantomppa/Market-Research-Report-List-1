High voltage leadless damped wafer resistors offer compactness and stability. High voltage cermet damping resistors combine ceramic and metallic materials, providing durability. Oxide film high voltage high-frequency resistors are effective in high-frequency applications, while high voltage bar glass glaze film resistors and sheet high voltage glass glaze film resistors utilize glass glaze for durability and thermal stability in high voltage environments.</p></p>
