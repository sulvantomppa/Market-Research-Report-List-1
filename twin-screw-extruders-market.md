<p><strong>The Twin-screw Extruders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Co-Rotating</li><li>Counter Rotating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Twin-screw extruders are categorized into two main types: co-rotating and counter-rotating. Co-rotating extruders consist of twin screws that rotate in the same direction, promoting uniform mixing and high shear, ideal for processing complex materials. In contrast, counter-rotating extruders have screws that rotate in opposite directions, facilitating a strong intermeshing of materials, suitable for applications requiring intensive mixing. Both types are widely utilized in plastics, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, each offering unique advantages based on the processing requirements.</p></p>
