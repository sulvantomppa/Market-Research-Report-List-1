<p><ul><li>Analytical Ultracentrifuge</li><li>Preparative Ultracentrifuge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop ultracentrifuge market comprises two primary types: analytical ultracentrifuges and preparative ultracentrifuges. Analytical ultracentrifuges are designed for high-resolution measurements and analysis of macromolecules, enabling researchers to study sedimentation behavior and molecular interactions in detail. In contrast, preparative ultracentrifuges focus on the separation and purification of biological samples, such as proteins and nucleic acids, facilitating larger-scale processing required in laboratories and biopharmaceutical industries. Together, these instruments play essential roles in advanced scientific research and applications.</p></p>
