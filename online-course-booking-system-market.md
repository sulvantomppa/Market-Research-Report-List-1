<p><p>Online Course Booking Systems can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and automatic updates, making them ideal for institutions looking for flexibility and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises systems are installed locally on an institution's servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring higher maintenance and initial investment. Each type caters to different institutional needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
