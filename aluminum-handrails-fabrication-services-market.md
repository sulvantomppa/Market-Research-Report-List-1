<p><p>Aluminum handrails fabrication services can be categorized into welded and non-welded markets. Welded handrails involve the permanent joining of aluminum components through welding techniques, ensuring strong and durable structures suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, non-welded handrails utilize mechanical fasteners, making them easier to assemble and disassemble, with flexibility in design. Each market type serves different needs, with welded options favored for robustness, while non-welded solutions appeal to those requiring adaptability and ease of installation.</p></p>
