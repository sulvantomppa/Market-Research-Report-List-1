<p><p>Magnetic reed proximity sensors are devices that detect the presence of a magnetic field, utilizing a pair of contacts sealed in a glass tube. The market features two primary types: analog and digital. Analog type sensors provide a continuous output proportional to the magnetic field strength, making them suitable for applications requiring precise measurements. In contrast, digital type sensors deliver a binary output—either on or off—triggered by the magnetic field, ideal for simple presence detection tasks in automation and security systems.</p></p>
