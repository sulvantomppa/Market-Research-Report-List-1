<p><p>The evacuation chair market consists of two primary types based on their stair navigation capabilities: descending and ascending chairs. Descending chairs are designed to facilitate the safe and controlled descent of individuals down stairs, ensuring stability and comfort. In contrast, ascending chairs enable users to move upwards, providing a solution for evacuation in multi-story buildings where stairs are the only exit. Both types cater to the needs of individuals with mobility challenges during emergencies, enhancing safety and accessibility.</p></p>
