<p><strong>The Handheld Multimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Analog Multimeter"</li><li>"Digital Multimeter"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld multimeter market is primarily categorized into two types: analog multimeters and digital multimeters. Analog multimeters display measurements using a moving needle on a dial, offering a simple and intuitive interface for users. They are generally more affordable but can be less precise. In contrast, digital multimeters provide numerical readouts on a digital display, offering higher accuracy, advanced features, and greater convenience for complex measurements. Both types cater to various user needs in electronics, automotive, and home applications.</p></p>
