<p><ul><li>Air Control Dental Chair</li><li>Electronic Control Dental Chair</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifunctional dental chair market comprises two main types: air control dental chairs and electronic control dental chairs. Air control dental chairs utilize pneumatic systems for adjusting height and positioning, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, electronic control dental chairs feature motorized adjustments, allowing for precise positioning and enhanced patient comfort with the push of a button. Both types are designed to improve efficiency during dental procedures, enhance ergonomics for practitioners, and provide a better overall experience for patients.</p></p>
