<p><p>The semiconductor transfer pumps market consists of various types, each specialized for specific applications. Diaphragm pumps are known for their versatility and leak-free operation, ideal for corrosive fluids. Peristaltic pumps offer precision and clean handling, suitable for delicate materials. Magnetic pumps utilize magnetic fields for frictionless operation, minimizing contamination risk. Bellows pumps provide excellent sealing and are used for high-purity applications. Other types may include gear, screw, or centrifugal pumps, expanding the options for diverse semiconductor manufacturing needs.</p></p>
