<p><p>The ductless mini-split heat pump market consists of two primary components: outdoor compressors and indoor air-handling units. The outdoor compressor is responsible for heat exchange, drawing in air and processing it to either release heat or cool the indoor environment. The indoor air-handling unit, installed within the living space, distributes the conditioned air efficiently without the need for ductwork. This system provides energy-efficient heating and cooling while allowing for individualized temperature control in different rooms.</p></p>
