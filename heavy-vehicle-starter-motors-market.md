<p><strong>The Heavy Vehicle Starter Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial (Sliding Armature)</li><li>Coaxial (Sliding Gear)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy vehicle starter motors market features two key types: axial (sliding armature) and coaxial (sliding gear). Axial starter motors utilize a sliding armature that moves along the axis to engage the drive gear, providing efficient torque and compact design. Coaxial starter motors, on the other hand, use a sliding gear mechanism that aligns with the engine's flywheel for effective engagement. Both types enhance starting performance in heavy vehicles, catering to varying design and application needs in the commercial transport sector.</p></p>
