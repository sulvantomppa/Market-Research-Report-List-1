<p><p>Ointment mills are essential in the pharmaceutical and cosmetic industries for achieving a uniform particle size in ointments and creams. There are two primary types: standard mills and ceramic mills. Standard mills utilize steel rollers to process materials, offering efficiency for various formulations. In contrast, ceramic mills use ceramic rollers, delivering superior quality and reduced contamination risks, making them ideal for sensitive applications. Both types cater to different needs, emphasizing performance, hygiene, and product integrity in ointment production.</p></p>
