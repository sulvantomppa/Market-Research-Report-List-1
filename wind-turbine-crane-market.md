<p><p>The wind turbine crane market includes various crane types essential for the construction and maintenance of wind turbines. Crawler cranes are tracked vehicles designed for stability on uneven terrain, providing high lifting capacities and mobility for heavy turbine components. Folding cranes, on the other hand, feature a compact design with articulated arms, allowing for easy maneuverability in tight spaces and quick setup. Both types play crucial roles in efficiently handling large turbine parts, ensuring safe and effective installation and servicing.</p></p>
