<p><p>The smoke control damper market encompasses various types designed for fire and smoke management in buildings. Mechanical dampers operate using mechanical systems to open or close airflow. Intumescent dampers expand and seal off smoke paths when exposed to heat. Air transfer fire dampers allow airflow while preventing smoke spread during a fire. Other types include gravity dampers and pressure relief dampers, each serving specific functions in maintaining safety and compliance in HVAC systems and building regulations.</p></p>
