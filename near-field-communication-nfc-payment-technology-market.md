<p><p>Near Field Communication (NFC) payment technology encompasses three primary market types. NFC Card Emulation allows mobile devices to mimic cards for transactions, enabling seamless payments at contactless terminals. NFC Reader/Writer enables devices to read data from NFC tags or write data to them, facilitating interactions like ticketing or loyalty programs. NFC Peer-to-Peer allows two devices to exchange data, enabling transactions or transfers between users. Together, these types enhance digital payments and connectivity in various applications.</p></p>
