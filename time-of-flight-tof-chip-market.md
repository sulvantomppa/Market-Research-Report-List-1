<p><p>The Time-of-Flight (ToF) chip market is primarily segmented into two types: indirect and direct ToF. Indirect ToF uses techniques like pulse modulation or phase shift to measure the time light takes to travel to an object and back, making it suitable for various applications, including consumer electronics. Direct ToF, on the other hand, utilizes laser-based methods for precise distance measurements, often employed in automotive and industrial sectors for advanced sensing capabilities and enhanced safety features.</p></p>
