<p><p>The Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) systems market features two key types: Conventional and Hybrid ZLD systems. Conventional ZLD systems focus on complete evaporation of water to minimize waste, typically using thermal methods, making them energy-intensive. Hybrid ZLD systems combine multiple technologies, such as membrane processes and evaporation, to enhance efficiency and reduce energy consumption. These systems are ideal for industries seeking sustainability while managing wastewater, ensuring minimal environmental impact and compliance with regulatory requirements.</p></p>
