<p><strong>The Optical-grade PMMA Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast PMMA Sheet</li><li>Extruded PMMA Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical-grade PMMA sheet market is divided into two main types: cast PMMA sheets and extruded PMMA sheets. Cast PMMA sheets are produced through a polymerization process, offering superior optical clarity and surface quality, making them ideal for applications like lenses and display screens. In contrast, extruded PMMA sheets are manufactured by forcing molten polymer through a die, resulting in lower production costs and a variety of thicknesses, suitable for less demanding applications such as signage and light fixtures.</p></p>
