<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Mapping System</li><li>Contact Mapping System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrophysiological 3D mapping systems market is categorized into two main types: non-contact mapping systems and contact mapping systems. Non-contact mapping systems utilize remote sensing technologies to create detailed voltage distributions of the heart without direct contact, allowing for rapid data acquisition and visualization. In contrast, contact mapping systems involve direct electrode contact with the cardiac tissue, providing high-resolution data on electrical activity and enabling precise ablation procedures. Both systems are crucial for diagnosing and treating arrhythmias effectively.</p></p>
