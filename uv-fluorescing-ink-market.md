<p><p>The UV fluorescing ink market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based inks. Solvent-based inks, known for their strong adhesion and vibrant colors, are commonly used in industrial applications and outdoor signage. They offer durability but can emit volatile organic compounds. Water-based inks, on the other hand, are environmentally friendlier and ideal for indoor applications, providing ease of cleanup and lower health risks. Both types are utilized for special effects in packaging, security printing, and promotional materials.</p></p>
