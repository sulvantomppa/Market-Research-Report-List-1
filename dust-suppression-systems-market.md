<p><p>The dust suppression systems market is primarily categorized into dry and wet systems. Dry dust suppression systems utilize methods such as vacuum collection and air filtration to minimize airborne particles without adding moisture. These are often favored in environments where water usage is limited. Conversely, wet dust suppression systems employ water, chemical agents, or foam to capture and settle dust particles. These systems are effective in various applications, including mining and construction, where moisture can enhance dust control efficacy.</p></p>
