Interior parts coatings enhance the appearance and resistance of components like dashboards and trim. Exterior parts coatings are specifically formulated for components such as bumpers and moldings, offering protection against weathering and corrosion. Refinish coatings are used for repairing and repainting vehicles, restoring their original look and protecting surfaces from wear and tear.</p></p>
