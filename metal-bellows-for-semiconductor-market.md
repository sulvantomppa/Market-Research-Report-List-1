<p><p>In the semiconductor market, metal bellows are crucial for providing flexibility and vacuum sealing in various applications. Edge welded metal bellows are constructed by welding edges of metal sheets, offering high durability and a wide range of sizes for dynamic applications. Electroformed metal bellows, on the other hand, are fabricated through an electroplating process, providing precise dimensions and excellent mechanical properties, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision and minimal outgassing. Both types ensure reliable performance in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
