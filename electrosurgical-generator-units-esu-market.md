<p><p>Electrosurgical Generator Units (ESUs) are devices that generate high-frequency electrical currents used in surgical procedures for cutting, coagulating, or vaporizing tissue. The market is segmented into three types: Monopole Electrosurgical Generators, which utilize a single active electrode for cutting and coagulation; Bipolar Electrosurgical Generators, which have two closely spaced electrodes allowing for precise tissue manipulation with minimal thermal spread; and Vessel Sealing Generators, specifically designed for sealing blood vessels effectively through hemostatic techniques.</p></p>
