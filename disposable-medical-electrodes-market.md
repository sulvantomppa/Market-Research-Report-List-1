Patch electrodes are adhesive, surface-mounted devices that facilitate non-invasive monitoring and are widely used in procedures like ECGs. Other types include specialized electrodes used for specific applications, such as intravascular monitoring or neuromuscular stimulation. These products are crucial for ensuring hygiene and preventing cross-contamination in medical settings.</p></p>
