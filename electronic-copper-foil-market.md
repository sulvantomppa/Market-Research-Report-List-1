<p><strong>The Electronic Copper Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic copper foil market is primarily categorized into two types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced by mechanically rolling copper into thin sheets, offering high precision and uniform thickness, making it suitable for various applications, including flexible printed circuit boards (PCBs). On the other hand, electrolytic copper foil is created through an electroplating process, leading to thicker options ideal for high-performance applications like batteries and connectors. Both types serve critical roles in the electronics industry.</p></p>
