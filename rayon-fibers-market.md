<p><p>The rayon fibers market encompasses two primary types: viscose staple fiber and viscose filament fiber. Viscose staple fiber, derived from regenerated cellulose, is widely used in textiles, apparel, and home furnishings due to its softness and versatility. In contrast, viscose filament fiber is utilized in producing high-quality fabrics that exhibit smoothness and luster, making it ideal for luxury clothing and upholstery. Both types cater to growing demand in the fashion and home textile industries, reflecting eco-friendly fiber preferences.</p></p>
