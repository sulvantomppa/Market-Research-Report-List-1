<p><strong>The Reverse Osmosis (RO) Artificial Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellulose-based Membrane</li><li>Thin film Composite Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Reverse Osmosis (RO) artificial membrane market comprises primarily two types: cellulose-based membranes and thin film composite membranes. Cellulose-based membranes, derived from natural polymers, offer good permeability but may have limitations in chemical resistance and durability. In contrast, thin film composite membranes, which consist of a polymeric layer on a supporting material, provide superior rejection rates, enhanced flux, and greater resistance to fouling, making them widely preferred in various water treatment applications. Both types play essential roles in filtration technology.</p></p>
