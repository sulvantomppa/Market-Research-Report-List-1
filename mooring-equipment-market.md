<p><p>The mooring equipment market includes various types essential for securing vessels. Mooring winches facilitate controlled tensioning of mooring lines. Anchor windlasses are used for raising and lowering anchors. Chain stoppers secure the anchor chain in place, preventing unwanted movement. Fairleads guide mooring lines, ensuring proper alignment and reducing wear. Capstans assist with manual hauling of ropes and lines. Additionally, "Others" category encompasses auxiliary equipment that supports mooring operations, enhancing efficiency and safety in maritime activities.</p></p>
